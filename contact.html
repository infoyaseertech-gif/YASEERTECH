Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get asked most often.

How do I get started with YaseerTech? +
Simply send us a WhatsApp message or fill the contact form above. Tell us what you need and we'll get back to you with a tailored plan and quote within 24 hours.
How much do your services cost? +
Our pricing depends on the scope of your project. We offer affordable packages for every budget. Contact us for a free custom quote tailored to your specific needs.
Do you offer ICT training for complete beginners? +
Yes! Our training programs are designed for all levels — from complete beginners to those looking to upgrade existing skills. We teach in a practical, easy-to-follow way.
How long does it take to build a website? +
A standard business website typically takes 5–10 working days. E-commerce sites and more complex projects may take 2–4 weeks depending on requirements.
Can you manage my social media and run ads for me? +
Absolutely. We offer full social media management and Meta Ads management services. We handle everything — strategy, content, ad setup, targeting, and monthly reporting.
Do you work with clients outside Abuja? +
Yes! While our office is in Wuse 2, Abuja, we work with clients across Nigeria and beyond. Most of our services are delivered digitally so location is no barrier.
