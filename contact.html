Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a website project take?
Most websites are completed within 1–3 weeks depending on complexity and how quickly you provide content and feedback.
Do you work with clients outside Abuja?
Yes! We work with clients across Nigeria and internationally. All communication is done online via WhatsApp, email, and video calls.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and cash. A 50% deposit is required to begin any project, with the balance on completion.
Do you offer support after the project?
Yes, we offer 30 days free support after every project delivery. Extended support plans are also available monthly.
Can you redesign my existing website?
Absolutely. We specialise in redesigning outdated websites into modern, mobile-friendly, high-converting platforms.
How much does a website cost?
Pricing depends on your requirements. Contact us for a free quote — we have packages suitable for startups, SMEs, and large businesses.
